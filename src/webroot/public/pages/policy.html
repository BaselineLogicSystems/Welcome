Privacy Policy

Our commitment to transparency and data minimization.

1. Introduction

At Baseline Logic Systems, LLC, we value your privacy and believe in total transparency. This policy specifically outlines how we handle data collected through our Driving Instructor Performance surveys.

2. Information We Collect

To maintain your privacy we collect only the following:

  • Voluntary Feedback: Any names, ratings, or comments you explicitly provide in the survey form.
  • Technical Identifiers: To prevent duplicate submissions and ensure system security, we may collect a temporary Session ID and an anonymized IP address.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • Instructor Improvement: Feedback is shared with the driving instructor and management to improve training quality and safety.
  • Analytics & Security: Anonymized IP addresses and session IDs are used purely for security auditing (e.g., preventing bot submissions) and basic analytics to ensure the survey is functioning correctly across different devices.

4. Data Sharing & Selling

Consistent with our company-wide policy, we do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Your feedback is used internally for quality assurance. We only share data with essential service providers who are strictly prohibited from using your data for any purpose other than providing the contracted technical services.

5. Your Rights & Control

  • Anonymity: You may choose to leave the "Name" field blank to remain anonymous.
  • Data Requests: If you wish to know what data we have on you, or request that your feedback be deleted from our records, please contact Baseline Logic Systems, LLC.
